What Is The RDBMS

A relational database management system (RDBMS or just RDB) is a common type of database that stores data in tables,so it can be used in relation to other stored datasets. Relational Database Management System having the ability to provide the Relationship between Two Different Tables. The data is often stored in many tables, also called ‘relations’. These tables are divided into rows, also called records and columns (fields). There can be millions of rows in a database. Columns are made up of one specific data type, like name or price.
